# Hub Pages

Hub pages aggregate notes referencing a term.

Example:

Artificial Intelligence.md

---

# Structure

Hub pages contain:

YAML frontmatter
Backlink list

Example:

---
title: Artificial Intelligence
created: Fri, 10 Jan 2026 19:00:00 UTC
---

## Backlinks to Artificial Intelligence

_Mentions: 4_

- [[AI Research]]
- [[Neural Networks]]
- [[Machine Learning History]]

---

# Markers

Hub pages use marker blocks:

<!-- VAULT_LINKER_HUB_START -->
<!-- VAULT_LINKER_HUB_END -->

These allow Vault-Linker to update backlinks safely.
